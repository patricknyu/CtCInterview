Chapter 5
========
Bit Manipulation
---------------

Here are the bit manipulation problems from the beginning of the chapter


1. 0110 + 0010 = 1000
2. 0011 + 0010 = 0101
3. 0110 - 0011 = 0011
4. 1000 - 0110 = 0010
5. 0011 * 0101 = 1111
6. 0011 * 0011 = 1001
7. 1101 >> 2 = 0011
  * **note** >> is a logical shift
8. 1101 ^ 0101 = 1000
  * **note** ^ is XOR
9. 0110 + 0110 = 1100
  * **note** this can be solved by doing 0110 * 2
10. 0100 * 0011 = 1100
11. 1101 ^ (~1101) = 1111
  * **note** ~ is the not/negation operation
12. 1011 & (~0 <<2) = 1000
  * **note** & is the and operation of big carrot
